Mercy Designs

Hello

I'm Mercy an entry-level interior designer with hands-on experience in space planning, material selection and creating 2D and 3D renders to deliver functionality and aesthetics that stay within budget constraints. I believe spaces shape how we feel. It’s more than just shelter - it’s atmosphere, memory and mood.

Services

Transforming ideas into lively experiences.

Residential Interior Design

Creating homes that balance beauty, comfort, and functionality. From space planning to furniture selection and realistic 3D visualization, I design interiors that reflect your lifestyle while making the most of every square meter.

Commercial Space Design

Designing spaces that elevate customer experience. Whether it's a café, office, or retail environment, I create interiors that are welcoming, practical, and aligned with your brand while maximizing flow and usability.

3D Visualization & Space Planning

Helping clients see their space before construction begins. Using ArchiCAD, SketchUp, Coohom, and Twinmotion, I produce floor plans, construction drawings, and photorealistic renders that communicate every design detail clearly. This aligns well with the software and technical skills highlighted in your portfolio.
